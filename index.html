FAQ

Do you guarantee suppliers / results?

I provide a qualified shortlist and a risk‑reduction process (samples, terms, inspection options). Final decisions and contracts remain yours.

Can you help with compliance (CE/FCC/ROHS)?

Yes — I’ll clarify requirements with suppliers and flag risks. Formal certification/testing is handled by accredited labs when needed.

What if I’m not sure about Incoterms (FOB/DDP)?

That’s common. I’ll explain options and suggest a safe shipping approach based on your market and timeline.
